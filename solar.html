About the Project

A comprehensive hybrid renewable energy charging station with integrated security

🌬️

Wind Energy System

A small-scale wind turbine converts kinetic energy from wind into AC electricity, which is then rectified to DC. The output varies with wind speed (v) following the power equation P = ½ρAv³, where ρ is air density and A is the swept area. This powers the battery bank and charging circuit when solar input is low.

☀️

Solar Energy System

Polycrystalline solar panels with a peak efficiency of ~17% convert photon energy into DC power via the photovoltaic effect. A Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) algorithm within the charge controller ensures optimal energy harvest throughout the day, adapting to varying irradiance levels.

⚡

Hybrid Charging Mechanism

Both wind and solar sources feed into a dual-input charge controller that manages priority, load balancing, and overcharge protection. The hybrid topology ensures continuous charging operation regardless of weather conditions, with failover between sources and a step-down converter for regulated 5V/12V output.

🔒

Anti-Theft Protection System

An IR proximity sensor mounted at the station detects unauthorized access. Upon detection, the Arduino UNO triggers the SIM800L GSM module to send an SMS alert to registered administrators. A relay module can physically lock the charging port, and all events are logged with timestamps to the backend database.

Mission & Objectives
01
Design and implement a hybrid wind-solar charging station capable of delivering stable power output under varying environmental conditions.
02
Develop an anti-theft protection system using IR sensing, relay-controlled locking, and automated SMS alerts via GSM module.
03
Build a real-time IoT monitoring platform that displays battery voltage, energy input from each source, and system health status.
04
Create a secure web application with role-based access control for administrators to manage the system remotely.
05
Demonstrate the viability of off-grid renewable energy charging as a sustainable and scalable alternative to conventional power sources.
06
Log all security events and energy metrics to a persistent database for historical analysis and performance optimization.
